Gaeng Ped

Gaend Ped is a red thai curry dish.

Ingredients​

  • Red curry paste
  • Tomato paste
  • Bambu shoots
  • Kafir lemon leaves
  • Thai basil leaves
  • (Optional) Dried shitake mushrooms

Instructions​

Cooking​

  • Add small portion of coconut milk to red curry paste. Add tomato paste and let cook.
  • Serve with rice.
