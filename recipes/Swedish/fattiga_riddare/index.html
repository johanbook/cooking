Fattiga Riddare

Fattiga Riddare, also known as arme Ritter in Germany, is a sweet breakfast dish. It is perfect for using up stale bread.

Ingredients​

  • Bread
  • Plant-based milk (I recommend vanilla-flavored one)
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom

Instructions​

  • Mix sugar, cinnamon and cardamom
  • Put flour into bowl and gradually add the milk while whisking
  • Cut bread slices in diaginal and dip in milk
  • Fry in vegan butter
