# Pad Thai

Pad Thai is a classical noodle stir fry that typically derives its sweet taste
from tamarind.

## Ingredients

- Rise noodles
- Red onion
- Clove of garlic
- Garlic chive
- Tamarind paste
- Palm sugar
- Soy sauce
- Bean sprouts (optional)

Garnish

- Roasted peanuts
- Lime
- Leaves of coriander (optional)
